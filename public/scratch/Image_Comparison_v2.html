🖼 Image Comparison — Quick Guide

Drop images onto a panel (or click the panel background) to load them. Click a thumbnail to show it on that side. Drag the centre divider to compare.

Mirror mode: both panels share one list. Independent mode: each panel has its own list.

Keyboard

← / →Move slider ±5%
+ / =Zoom in
− (minus)Zoom out
0Reset zoom & pan
Double-click VPReset zoom & pan
FToggle fullscreen
EscapeExit fullscreen

Zoom & Pan

Scroll wheel to zoom (centred on cursor). Click-drag to pan when zoomed in. Pinch-to-zoom on touch.

Fullscreen

Move your cursor to the far left or right edge to slide in that panel. It auto-hides 2 s after your cursor leaves. Move to the bottom edge to reveal the toolbar.
